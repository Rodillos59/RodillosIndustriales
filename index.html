How We Work

  1. Send files: STEP, DXF, or PDF drawings (or photos + measurements if needed).
  2. We quote fast: scope, material, tolerances, and lead time confirmed.
  3. We manufacture/repair: in-process inspection using micrometers and calipers.
  4. Delivery: cross-border coordination for U.S. customers when required.

To speed quoting, include quantity, target delivery date, and any critical dimensions.

FAQ

What files do you accept for quoting?

STEP, DXF, and PDF drawings are preferred. If you do not have drawings, send clear photos and key measurements.

What is your typical lead time?

Many jobs are completed in 3–5 business days depending on scope, materials, and queue.

Do you support U.S. customers with cross-border logistics?

Yes. We routinely support cross-border jobs and can coordinate delivery expectations with U.S. customers.

Can you handle emergency repairs?

Yes, when capacity allows. Include urgency and downtime impact in your request so we can prioritize appropriately.
