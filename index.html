FAQ

Would you consider Mexicali “nearshore” for U.S. companies?

Yes. In manufacturing, “nearshore” commonly means moving work closer to the U.S. versus overseas. Mexicali is a near-border manufacturing hub that supports fast turnaround and easier coordination.

What files do you accept for quoting?

STEP, DXF, and PDF drawings are preferred. If you do not have drawings, send clear photos and key measurements.

What is your typical lead time?

Many jobs are completed in 3–5 business days depending on scope, materials, and queue.

Can you handle emergency repairs?

Yes, when capacity allows. Include urgency and downtime impact in your request so we can prioritize appropriately.
